About Project

About ResumeAnalyzer

ResumeAnalyzer is an AI-powered career tool that enhances your job application process from start to finish. From analyzing resumes and generating custom cover letters to preparing you for interviews, our platform is designed to help you confidently apply to any role.
We prioritize privacy—uploaded files are auto-deleted within minutes. No personal data is stored.

What I Do?
  1. ResumeAnalyzer analyzes your resume against job descriptions, providing keyword matches, skill gaps, and an ATS score.
  2. My Resume Builder helps you craft a new resume or refine your current one using a guided Q&A approach.
  3. Need a cover letter? Input your resume and job role—we’ll generate a custom version instantly.
  4. My Mock Interview tool helps you practice with AI-generated questions tailored to your profile.
Why Choose ResumeAnalyzer?

I built ResumeAnalyzer to make job applications smarter, faster, and stress-free. Whether you’re a student, fresher, mid-level employee, or experienced professional, our tools adapt to your needs.

For Whome?
  1. Interns: Create resumes that highlight your potential, not just experience.
  2. Freshers: Emphasize academics, projects, and strengths to stand out.
  3. Mid-Level Professionals: Present growth and achievements effectively.
  4. Experienced Candidates: Optimize long careers into concise, impactful resumes.
My Goal

To empower job seekers with intelligent, easy-to-use tools that make resume building, reviewing, and applying intuitive and efficient.
